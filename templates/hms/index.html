Meet Our Founder

Leadership & Vision

Madan Mohan Chilkoti

Founder & Proprietor

Building Trust Through Excellence

With over 15 years of dedication to automotive excellence, Madan Mohan Chilkoti founded Gaurav Motors in 2010 with a vision to bring world-class car care services to Lohaghat, Uttarakhand.

Under his leadership, Gaurav Motors has grown from a small workshop to Uttarakhand's most trusted automotive service center, serving over 5,000 satisfied customers. His commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has set new standards in the region.

To be Uttarakhand's most trusted automotive care provider through innovation and excellence.
