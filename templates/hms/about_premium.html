OUR JOURNEY

From a Small Garage to Uttarakhand's Best

Gaurav Motors started in 2010 with a simple vision: provide honest, reliable, and professional car service to the people of Lohaghat.

Over 15 years, we've grown from a small garage into a fully-equipped modern workshop with the latest diagnostic tools, certified technicians, and a commitment to excellence that has earned us the trust of over 5000 customers.
