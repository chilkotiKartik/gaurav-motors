Our Journey

Established in 2010 with a commitment to deliver premium automotive care to Uttarakhand.

Gaurav Motors started as a small garage in Lohaghat with a vision to provide honest, reliable, and professional car service. Over the years, we've grown into the region's most trusted automotive care center, serving over 5000+ satisfied customers with modern equipment and certified technicians.

Our journey is guided by a simple principle: "Your vehicle, Our priority." This commitment drives us to continuously upgrade our skills, invest in the latest diagnostic tools, and maintain the highest standards of service quality.
